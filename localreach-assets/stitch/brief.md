# Plumber Brno V1

## Goal
Create a believable Czech local plumber landing page for outreach screenshots and future reuse as a niche template inside LocalReach.

## Business type
Instalatér / havarijní instalatér v Brně

## Audience
People in Brno who need urgent plumbing help, repairs, installation, or water/heating related service.

## What works in the Stitch design
- Clear hero
- Strong CTA buttons
- Good section order
- Trust metrics under hero
- Contact form included
- Testimonials included

## Weak points
- Too generic / template-like
- Background motif feels slightly artificial
- Icons and service cards are fine but not very distinctive
- Some text blocks feel placeholder-level
- Lower sections need more believable Czech local-business detail
- Footer is weak
- Overall still looks more “AI-generated template” than a real small-business website

## Implementation goal
Use the structure and hierarchy, but port it into a more believable LocalReach niche template with stronger Czech realism and better screenshot credibility.
